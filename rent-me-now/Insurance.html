What is Car Insurance?

Car insurance could cover costs if your car is damaged in an accident,stolen or catches fire. You're legally required to have it if you drive or keep your car on a public road.The minimum level of car insurance by law is third party insurance - this could cover you if you injure someone else or damage their car, but your level of cover and exacly what you're covered for depends on the policy you chose.

What does Rent Me Now Insurance Cover?
